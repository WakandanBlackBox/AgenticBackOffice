# Google Gemini Video Generation Prompt -- BackOffice Agent Demo

Upload ALL of the referenced screenshots to Gemini alongside this prompt. The screenshots are described below in the order they should appear in the video.

## PROMPT

Create a polished, professional 60-90 second product demo video for "BackOffice Agent" -- an AI-powered back-office platform for freelancers. Use the uploaded screenshots as the visual foundation, applying smooth Ken Burns-style pan and zoom animations to bring each screen to life. The video should feel like a modern SaaS product launch trailer.

### Visual Style & Mood

- **Color palette:** Dark navy/midnight blue UI (#060A14 background, #2563EB blue accents, white/light gray text). The app has a premium, high-contrast dark theme. Maintain this mood throughout.
- **Motion style:** Slow, cinematic zooms into key areas of each screenshot. Smooth crossfade transitions between screens (0.5-0.8s). Subtle parallax depth effects on UI elements where possible.
- **Text overlays:** Use clean, modern sans-serif font (similar to Plus Jakarta Sans or Inter). White text with subtle blue glow for emphasis keywords. Place overlay text in empty space areas of screenshots, never covering key UI elements.
- **Aspect ratio:** 16:9, 1080p
- **Pacing:** Deliberate and confident. Each screen gets 8-12 seconds of attention. Never rush.

### Audio & Music

- Background music: Upbeat but not aggressive electronic/ambient track. Think "confident tech startup" energy. Soft synth pads with a subtle beat. Similar vibe to Stripe or Linear product videos.
- No voiceover (text overlays carry the narrative instead). If voiceover is supported, use a calm, confident male or female voice reading the overlay text naturally.

### Key Details to Emphasize

These are the product's strongest selling points. Make sure the video highlights them:

1. **Five AI Agents** -- Proposal, Invoice, Contract, Scope Guardian, and Insight agents working together
2. **Multi-agent coordination** -- One prompt triggers multiple agents that pass context to each other
3. **Scope creep protection** -- The Scope Guardian agent detects scope creep and calculates change orders automatically
4. **Milestone-based billing** -- Kanban board with approval gates, sequential milestone flow
5. **Cost transparency** -- Every AI interaction costs roughly a penny, with full token/cost tracking in the Activity Log
6. **Client portal** -- One-click shareable link for clients to approve milestones (mentioned in text overlay, not shown in screenshots)
7. **Dark premium aesthetic** -- The entire UI has a modern, high-end dark theme with blue accents

### What NOT to Do

- Do not add cheesy stock footage or generic B-roll. The screenshots ARE the content.
- Do not add flashy, distracting transitions (no star wipes, no 3D flips). Keep it clean and modern.
- Do not speed through screens. Let viewers absorb the UI.
- Do not add sound effects for clicks or typing unless they are very subtle and tasteful.
- Do not crop or distort the screenshots. Maintain their original aspect ratio and use pan/zoom to focus on regions.
- Do not use bright white backgrounds between scenes. Stay in the dark color palette.
